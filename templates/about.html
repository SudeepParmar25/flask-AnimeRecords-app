About

Welcome to Anime Records — your simple tool to track and explore anime titles with ease.

🎯 Our Mission

We aim to provide anime lovers with a clean, minimal interface to search, bookmark, and log the anime they love. Whether you're a seasoned fan or just starting, our platform is designed to make your experience better.

👥 Who Am i

Anime Records was created by a passionate developer and anime enthusiast as a personal project to learn full-stack development and build something meaningful for the community.

📜 Disclaimer

Anime Records does not host or stream any anime content directly. We simply link to third-party sites like Aniwatch and Hianime for convenience.

All logos, images, and content belong to their respective copyright holders. This site is made purely for educational and informational purposes and does not claim ownership of any external content.

If you are a copyright holder and want any material removed, please contact us immediately and we will comply.

📩 Contact

If you have any suggestions, questions, or concerns, feel free to reach out via the contact form or through GitHub (if made public).
